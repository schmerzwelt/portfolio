on your doorstep

illustration

On Your Doorstep is a film about the ecosystem of the generic back garden. It states that to find nature sometimes we have to look closer instead of far away. The film was made as the final dissertation of Sander van Iersel for his MA course in Wildlife Documentary production. The film was made with a frugal can-do mentality by a team of students who each put their distinct creative expertise into it.
