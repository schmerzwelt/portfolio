Preuvenemint 2014

graphic design

The Preuvenemint is an annual event held in Maastricht. It is the biggest open air restaurant of the Netherlands. At the Preuvenemint, entrepreneurs and chefs gather to serve all kinds of food and drinks. Despite being free to visit, the Preuvenemint is known for being more of a networking event for local business men and women. Regarding this year’s theme, 200 years of a Dutch Kingdom, I wanted to break with these prejudices, by creating an open, fresh and friendly design which is clear but still interesting to look at. The poster design was picked after a short pitch.
