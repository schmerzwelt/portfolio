The Wish

illustration | motion graphics

The Wish was a school assignment in which we had to translate an existing story to a short animation. Mine is losely based upon The Wish, a short story by Roald Dahl, in which a little boy crosses a carpet in the living room, and loses himself in his own imagination. Instead of a kid, my main character is a malamute pup, living in a cold and snowy landscape, together with his owner. When the pup decides to leave the house on his own, the wind starts blowing and he loses himself in another dimension, until his owner finds him laying under a plaid.
